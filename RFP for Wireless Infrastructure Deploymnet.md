# Request for Proposal for Wireless Infrastructure Deployment
#### November 20th, 2025

### About Us
Pinegins is company dedicated to producing premium pine-infused beverages. Founded on the dedication to customer satisfaction, Pinegins is known for its high quality and strong reputation. Spanning two continents and twelve countries, Pinegins combines traditional distilling techniques with modern flavoring, preserving the old while striving for the new.

To support our rapid growth, Pinegins is seeking proposals from qualified vendors about the deployment of a wireless infrastructure for our Winnipeg Office. Our goals is to enhance our wireless network speed, quaity, and accomodate the growing number of staff.

### Project Scope
__Objectives__
- Accomodate up to 1000 concurrent connections on the wireless network.
- Achieve a full signal coverage inside the Winnipeg Office (at least -67 dBm in all indoor areas). 
- Support a bandwidth of at least 5 Gbps for all users on the wireless network.
- Reducing the amount of personal devices using the wireless network by 200%.
- Minimize the risk of offline dictionary password guessing attacks on the wireless network.
- Improve network visibility to 100% with the implementation of diagnostic tools and dedicated staff. 

__Infrastructure Requirements__
- Support full coverage throughout the physical office premises of our Winnipeg Office.
- Support the latest wireless standard, Wi-Fi 6, for faster speeds and low latency. 
- Support more simultaneous connections to our wireless infrastructure from our employees, partners and guests.
- Improve our security posture with the implementation of security features, such as WPA3 and MAC address filtering. This seeks to secure data in transit and allow only authorized devices to connect to our network. 
- Deploy tools and capabilites for continuous network monitoring and threat detection. Such ensures that any anomalies or threats are identified and terminated before affecting our users and company. 

__Scope of Work__
- Identification of the most optimal placement for access points on site. Factors like Wi-Fi coverage, security and electromagnetic interference should be accounted for. 
- Installation and configuration of the new wireless access points throughout the Winnipeg Office.
- Ongoing maintenance and support for the new wireless infrastructure to ensure its successfull and uninterruped operation. 
- Administrators and Staff Operations Training.

### Technical Requirements
__Security and Compliance Requirements__
- Installation requirements: must provide a list of wireless access point vendors that the technicians have experience with.
- Endpoint security: provide a descriptive and detailed plan of the proposed solution, including the amount and type of wireless access points and the security settings that they support.
- Network security: must provide a list of solutions that may mitigate or protect against eavesdropping, man-in-the-middle wireless attacks, jamming, and rogue access points.
- Authentication: provide a descriptive plan on securing the authentication to the wireless network, such as implementing Multi-Factor Authentication and setting up a seperate guest network.
- All equipment must be certified by Innovation, Science and Economic Development Canada (ISED).
- All data encryption and protection implemented in the wireless network must adhere to ITSP.80.002. 


### Vendor Qualifications
__Experience__
- At least five years of experience designing and implementing wireless networks across different businesses in Canada.
  
__Certifications__
- Valid certifications such as RSP-100, TRC-43 AND TRC-49 must be provided.
- Technician certifications from the chosen wireless access point vendor is a plus. 

__References__
- Must provide at least two references from previous clients or businesses in Canada that had similair projects.
- Must provide contact information of the previous clients.


### Evaluation Criteria
- __Cost__: A detailed breakdown of costs, including the initial setup, cost of hardware, materials and labour. In addition, the cost of maintenance should also be included.
- __Technical Approach__: A descriptive explanation of how the technician team would implement the solution, including any needed equipment, time, work conditions and any possible interruptions to the Pinegins' staff work. 
- __Timeline__: A detailed project schedule, including important milestones, expected completion dates, and the shortes amount of time that the network will be unusable for.
